Cache Overview

The cache allows you to store items for quick retrieval. Items will persist for the life of the application, until you explicitly clear the cache by calling clear() or by removing an item with a particular key via removeItem(key)

Try it out below. Supply a key and a value, then retrieve that item from the cache. Try it with multiple keys for multiple values. Then try to clear the cache and get one of the keys you previously stored.
