Overview

The cache allows you to store items for quick retrieval. Items will persist for the life of the application and onwards, until you explicitly clear the cache by calling appworks.cache.clear() or by removing an item with a particular key with appworks.cache.removeItem(key)

Tips

Try it out below. Supply a key and a value, then retrieve that item from the cache. Try it with multiple keys for multiple values. Then try to clear the cache and get one of the keys you previously stored.
